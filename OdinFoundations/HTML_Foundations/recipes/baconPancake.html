Bacon pancakes

Ingredients

  • 12 Bacon Slices
  • 2 Cup of flour
  • 1/2 TSP of baking powder
  • 1/2 TSP of baking soda
  • 1 TSP of sugar
  • 1 TSP of salt
  • 2 Cups of milk
  • 1/2 stick of butter
  • 1 Eggs

Steps

  1. Whisk together the flour with baking powder, sugar, baking soda, and salt
  2. In another bowl mix milk, melted butter and egg
  3. Whisk the mixtures!
  4. Put the mix into a pan!
  5. take some bacon and put it in a pancake
